About UPES

University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) is a private university located in Dehradun, Uttarakhand, India. Established in 2003, UPES was the first Indian university to offer courses in the energy sector and has since become a leading institution in the fields of petroleum, energy, and allied sectors. UPES offers over 33 graduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various fields such as engineering, management, law, design, health sciences, and more. The university has a diverse student population of over 15,000 students from 90+ countries.
